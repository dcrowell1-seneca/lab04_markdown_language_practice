# Mustard Pickles

## Ingredients

- 1 head of Cauliflower
- 12 Cucumbers
- 3 lbs of white onions
- 1/2 cup of salt
- 1 1/3 cup of flour
- 7 cups of sugar
- 4 teaspoons tumeric
- 4 teasppons dried mustard
- 2 quarts of vinegar 

## Steps

1. Chop cumcumbers, cauliflower and onion
2. Mix the chopped vegetables
3. Sprinkle salt over the mix
4. Cover it and put in fridge over night
5. Mix flour, sugar, and tumeric, and dry mustard in a pot
6. Mix vinegar in with the resulting mixture from step 5
7. Put mixutre in pot and on stove and heat for medium heat until it thickens
8. Pour result from step 7 over vegetables
9. Simmer for 1/2 hour while stirring
10. Put the mustard pickles in jars


**Tip 1: Stir every 2-3 minutes while on the stove, otherwise it will burn**

**Tip 2: When bottling, make sure the bottles are sterilized and clean.**
